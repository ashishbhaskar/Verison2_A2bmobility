GTFS real time data includes simple text files as live feeds on vehicle positions, trip updates, and service alerts. Translink real time feeds are at the frequency of 2-10 seconds, the download for which is around 25 GB of data per day.  To give life to this big data in text format, we are developing number of visualization tools.
