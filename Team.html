About

Addressing urban mobility challenges with research focusing on road traffic congestion management and control. Active in:

  • Transport data analytics
  • Predictive modelling (traffic state estimation and prediction)
  • Traffic flow theory (emerging connected and automated vehicles; non-lane/quasi lane discipline)
  • Multimodal demand modelling (exploiting advance traffic and transit data)
  • Real time multimodal operations and control
  • Traffic simulation
  • Integrated network control
  • Benchmarking

Associate Prof Ashish Bhaskar

Dr Ashish Bhaskar is Associate Professor at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT), Brisbane, Australia. His academic career has a balanced workload of research, teaching and service role. He is:

  • Enhancing research capability in road traffic network operations and ITS in public transport
  • Developing research capacity in data science and its applications in transport
  • Engaging with industry for applied research and research transformed into practice
  • Collaborating with key inter and intra disciplinary academics.
